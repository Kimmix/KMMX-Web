I started to look around… It seems the door was locked from the inside… was it me who locked myself inside? In front of me was a blinking panel. I instinctively placed my hand on the panel. I feel a burst of Arcai energy flowing through me. Operation successful. You are now free. ███████████. The static noise was interfering as the voice cut off. The panel split apart as some sort of weapon came out. I pulled out the handle to reveal a katana. A spark of memory ran through my head… remembering that I was the owner of this katana. I started to foolishly swing the sword around. I tried to let go as it holstered magnetically to my back. Looking around for a way out of this place, it seemed the door was shut tight. I'll need to cut my way out; I pulled a katana from my back trying to cut down the door, but it didn't work. After a furious attempt later, holding my grip tight, I begin to feel Arcai energy flow through the katana as it begins to glow… hard light starts to form around me like some sort of armor. I took a single swing, and the door burst open.
